How DealKhaleej handles visitor information.

Effective 29 May 2026

Information We Collect

DealKhaleej may collect limited information that visitors choose to provide, such as an email address submitted through a newsletter form or a message sent to our contact address. We may also collect basic technical information through analytics tools, server logs, cookies, and similar technologies.

This technical information can include pages viewed, approximate location, device type, browser type, referral source, clicks on offers, and the date and time of visits. We use this information to understand site performance and improve coupon discovery.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to operate the website, send requested deal alerts, measure popular stores and categories, detect broken links, improve content, and protect the site from abuse. We do not sell personal contact information to third parties.

Affiliate links may send visitors to merchant or partner websites. Those websites have their own privacy practices, cookies, and tracking technologies. Please review the privacy policy of any merchant site before providing personal or payment information.

Cookies, Analytics, And Choices

DealKhaleej may use cookies or similar technologies for analytics, affiliate tracking, saved preferences, and feature performance. Most browsers allow visitors to block or delete cookies, although some features may not work as expected after cookies are disabled.

Newsletter subscribers can request removal from our email list by contacting hello@dealkhaleej.com. We may retain limited records when needed for security, legal compliance, or operational integrity.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy as the site changes. The effective date above shows the latest version. Continued use of DealKhaleej after changes are posted means the updated policy applies to future visits.
