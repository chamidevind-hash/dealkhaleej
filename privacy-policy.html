Privacy Policy

How DealKhaleej handles privacy.

Information We Use

DealKhaleej may use basic analytics, click activity, device information, and optional newsletter information to improve coupon listings, understand site performance, and maintain reliable deal pages.

If users contact DealKhaleej by email, we may use the provided email address and message content to respond to the request.

Cookies, Analytics, and Affiliate Links

DealKhaleej may use analytics and tracking tools to measure visits, clicks, and offer performance. Third-party stores or affiliate networks may also use cookies when users leave DealKhaleej through a deal link.

DealKhaleej may earn commissions when users purchase through affiliate links. This does not affect the prices paid by users.

Third-Party Stores

DealKhaleej links to external stores and services. Their privacy policies and checkout practices are controlled by those third parties, not DealKhaleej.

Updates

This policy may be updated as DealKhaleej adds new features, stores, or user communication options.
