How To Reach Us

For coupon corrections, store listing updates, partnership questions, or general feedback, email DealKhaleej at hello@dealkhaleej.com. Please include the store name, coupon code or offer title, and a short description of the issue so we can review it quickly.

If you are a merchant or affiliate partner, include your brand name, website, market coverage, and the type of offer you want listed. We review submissions for relevance to Saudi shoppers before adding or updating content.

Coupon Corrections

Online offers can expire, change conditions, or become limited to specific customers without advance notice. If a code fails at checkout, tell us where it failed and whether the merchant displayed any eligibility message. That context helps us decide whether to edit, remove, or relabel the offer.

Response Times

We try to review important corrections as soon as possible, especially expired codes or broken outbound links. Partnership and content requests may take longer because we check relevance, disclosure requirements, and page quality before publishing updates.

DealKhaleej does not handle merchant orders, shipping, payments, refunds, warranty claims, or account support. For those issues, contact the store where the purchase was made.
